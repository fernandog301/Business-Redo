Smokin' Roadhouse BBQ

Started by the Jones family in 1989 this amazing BBQ food truck has over 1,000 5 star reviews across the board. They are the best around when it comes to anything and everythingBBQ.

Pulled Pork Sandwich

Slow-smoked pulled pork piled high on a soft bun, topped with coleslaw and drizzled with tangy barbecue sauce.

Beef Brisket Platter

Tender slices of smoked beef brisket served with pickles, onions, and a side of barbecue sauce.

Spicy Jalapeño Brisket Melt

Sliced brisket, jalapeños, pepper jack cheese, and barbecue sauce on a toasted ciabatta roll.

Smokin' Roadhouse BBQ

Entrees:

  1. Pulled Pork Sandwich
    • Slow-smoked pulled pork piled high on a soft bun, topped with coleslaw and drizzled with tangy barbecue sauce.
  2. Beef Brisket Platter
    • Tender slices of smoked beef brisket served with pickles, onions, and a side of barbecue sauce.
  3. St. Louis Style Ribs
    • Fall-off-the-bone St. Louis-style ribs, dry-rubbed and glazed with a sweet and spicy barbecue sauce.

BBQ Sandwiches:

  1. Texas Toast BBQ Grilled Cheese
    • Smoked cheddar and pulled chicken or pork sandwiched between two slices of Texas toast, grilled to perfection.
  2. Spicy Jalapeño Brisket Melt
    • Sliced brisket, jalapeños, pepper jack cheese, and barbecue sauce on a toasted ciabatta roll.

BBQ Bowls:

  1. Smoked Chicken Mac 'n' Cheese Bowl
    • Smoked chicken over creamy mac 'n' cheese, topped with crispy fried onions.
  2. BBQ Loaded Potato Bowl
    • Smoked pulled pork, cheddar cheese, sour cream, and green onions over a loaded baked potato.

Sides and Snacks:

  1. Southern Cornbread Muffins
    • Sweet and savory cornbread muffins served with honey butter.
  2. Crispy BBQ Tater Tots
    • Tater tots tossed in barbecue seasoning, served with chipotle mayo.

BBQ Tacos:

  1. Smoked Brisket Tacos
    • Sliced brisket, pickled red onions, and cotija cheese in corn tortillas, drizzled with barbecue sauce.
  2. Pulled Chicken Street Tacos
    • Pulled chicken, pico de gallo, and avocado crema in small flour tortillas

Sabor Street Eats

Sabor Street Eats is the best all-around Mexican food truck in Stockton. They began serving during Covid and have had great success since.

Crowd Favorites

Carne Asada Tacos

Grilled marinated steak topped with diced onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.

Al Pastor Tacos

Spit-roasted pork with pineapple, onions, and fresh cilantro.

Flaming Hot Cheetos Elote

A spicy twist with crushed Flaming Hot Cheetos coating the elote.

Sabor Street Eats

Street Tacos:

  1. Carne Asada Tacos
    • Grilled marinated steak topped with diced onions, cilantro, and a squeeze of lime.
  2. Al Pastor Tacos
    • Spit-roasted pork with pineapple, onions, and fresh cilantro.
  3. Vegetarian Street Tacos
    • Grilled portobello mushrooms, bell peppers, and onions topped with queso fresco and salsa verde.

Quesadilla Delights:

  1. Chicken and Mole Quesadilla
    • Shredded chicken, rich mole sauce, melted cheese, and a sprinkle of sesame seeds.
  2. Spicy Shrimp Quesadilla
    • Sauteed shrimp, jalapeños, onions, and cheese folded in a crispy tortilla.

Burrito Bowls:

  1. Barbacoa Burrito Bowl
    • Slow-cooked barbacoa beef, cilantro-lime rice, black beans, pico de gallo, and guacamole.
  2. Chile Verde Burrito Bowl
    • Tender chunks of pork simmered in green chile sauce, served with rice, beans, and sour cream.

Mexican Street Corn (Elote):

  1. Classic Elote
    • Grilled corn on the cob slathered with mayo, cotija cheese, chili powder, and a lime wedge.
  2. Flaming Hot Cheetos Elote
    • A spicy twist with crushed Flaming Hot Cheetos coating the elote.

Wok-Star Express

Started in 2016, Wok-Star Express has been pure flavor since their viral video featuring the incredibly unique roasted duck bao buns!

Crowd Favorites

Roast Duck Bao

Tender slices of roast duck, hoisin sauce, green onions, and cucumber tucked into fluffy steamed bao buns.

Kung Pao Chicken

Spicy stir-fried chicken with peanuts, bell peppers, and chili peppers in a flavorful Kung Pao sauce.

Steamed Dumplings (Xiao Long Bao)

Delicate dumplings filled with pork, ginger, and a savory broth, served with black vinegar and ginger dipping sauce.

Wok-Star Express

Dim Sum Delights:

  1. Steamed Dumplings (Xiao Long Bao)
    • Delicate dumplings filled with pork, ginger, and a savory broth, served with black vinegar and ginger dipping sauce.
  2. Shrimp Har Gow
    • Translucent dumplings filled with succulent shrimp, bamboo shoots, and water chestnuts, served with soy sauce.

Bao Bun Bites:

  1. Roast Duck Bao
    • Tender slices of roast duck, hoisin sauce, green onions, and cucumber tucked into fluffy steamed bao buns.
  2. Szechuan Tofu Bao
    • Crispy tofu, Szechuan sauce, pickled vegetables, and cilantro in a soft bao bun.

Noodle Nirvana:

  1. Beef Chow Mein
    • Stir-fried egg noodles with marinated beef, bok choy, and mushrooms in a savory soy-based sauce.
  2. Vegetable Lo Mein
    • Soft noodles tossed with a medley of colorful vegetables and a light garlic-soy sauce.

Wok-Tossed Wonders:

  1. Kung Pao Chicken
    • Spicy stir-fried chicken with peanuts, bell peppers, and chili peppers in a flavorful Kung Pao sauce.
  2. General Tso's Tofu
    • Crispy tofu bites coated in a sweet and tangy General Tso's sauce, served with broccoli.
